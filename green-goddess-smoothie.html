Green Goddess Smoothie

A nutrient-dense powerhouse blend for sustained energy and mental clarity

3 Minutes
1 Serving
Low Difficulty
Keto Diet Type

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh organic spinach
  • ½ ripe avocado
  • 1 scoop unflavored collagen peptides
  • ½ cup full-fat coconut cream
  • 1 tablespoon MCT oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • ½ cucumber, peeled
  • 1 cup filtered water
  • 5-6 ice cubes
  • Stevia to taste (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add filtered water and coconut cream to your high-speed blender first for optimal blending.
  2. Add the fresh spinach and blend for 30 seconds until completely smooth.
  3. Add avocado, cucumber, ginger, and lemon juice. Blend for another 30 seconds.
  4. Add collagen peptides and blend briefly to incorporate without creating foam.
  5. Add MCT oil slowly while blending on low speed to emulsify properly.
  6. Add ice cubes and blend until desired consistency is reached.
  7. Taste and adjust with stevia if needed. Serve immediately in a chilled glass.

Benefits

  • MCT oil provides instant ketones for mental clarity and focus
  • Collagen peptides support muscle recovery and joint health
  • Spinach and cucumber provide natural detoxification compounds
  • Healthy fats provide long-lasting energy without crashes
  • Avocado delivers heart-healthy monounsaturated fats
  • Ginger aids digestion and reduces inflammation
  • Lemon juice boosts vitamin C and enhances iron absorption

Pro Tips

  • Use frozen spinach if fresh isn't available - it actually blends smoother
  • Start with less MCT oil if you're new to it to avoid digestive upset
  • Chill your glass in the freezer for 10 minutes before serving
  • Add a pinch of sea salt to enhance the flavors and provide electrolytes
  • For extra creaminess, freeze banana slices and add 2-3 pieces
  • Drink within 15 minutes for maximum nutrient absorption
