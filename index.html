Optimization Tips

Morning Routine

  • Start with 16-20 oz of filtered water
  • Wait 30 minutes before coffee
  • Add MCT oil gradually to avoid digestive upset
  • Practice intermittent fasting for metabolic benefits

Food Quality

  • Choose grass-fed and pastured animal products
  • Prioritize organic vegetables and fruits
  • Avoid processed and inflammatory foods
  • Source high-quality fats and oils

Timing & Preparation

  • Eat within a 6-8 hour window
  • Meal prep on weekends for consistency
  • Listen to your body's hunger signals
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day

Performance Tracking

  • Monitor energy levels and mental clarity
  • Track sleep quality and duration
  • Test biomarkers quarterly
  • Adjust based on individual response
